Faqs

Anyone currently pursuing an undergraduate degree from any institution in India, across all streams is eligible to participate in ZestX 2021. Alumnus of IIITM Gwalior and graduated students of any institute are not permitted to participate.

You can participate free of cost in some events, and some of them have registration fees. You can check their fees during the registration process.

You can participate in all events! We have not set any cap on the number of events you can participate in, and win too!

Unfortunately we do not have a campus ambassador program this year, but we will surely be having one next time!

The deadline for registering in an event is before the start of the event.

Yes. We will require the official institute/university email address issued to you. In case you do not have an institute/university email address, you will be required to send an email from any person having a valid institute/university email address to verify.zestx@gmail.com confirming that you are a student of the institution.

You can check list of ongoing/upcoming events and past events in which you have participated in your profile section.
